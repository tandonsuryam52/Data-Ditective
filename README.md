This Sales Performance Dashboard project demonstrates my ability to transform raw data into meaningful business insights using data visualization tools. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer distribution, and regional trends, enabling data-driven decision-making.

The total sales reached 393M, with an average sale value of 78.58K across 5000 customers, reflecting strong business performance. A detailed department-wise analysis highlights consistent contributions across Sales, Marketing, HR, Finance, and other departments, each maintaining a balanced share of overall revenue.

The category-wise sales breakdown identifies top-performing individuals, with Ankit Mishra leading at 103M, followed by Garima Singh and Mukesh Gupta. This helps in recognizing high performers and understanding contribution patterns.

From a geographical perspective, the regional sales distribution shows the South region leading with 21.42%, followed by East and West, indicating potential market strengths and opportunities for expansion.

The year-wise trend analysis reveals a slight dip in 2023 (129.5M) compared to 2022 (131.7M), followed by recovery in 2024 (131.8M), showcasing resilience and growth potential.

Data cleaning and transformation

Identifying trends and performance insights
Presenting data in a clear, impactful manner
